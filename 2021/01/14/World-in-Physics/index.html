heat capacity

$C$: the amount of energy needed to raise the temperature of that sample by $1$ degree.

heat transfer $Q=C\delta T$

$c$: specific heat capacity. the heat capacity per unit mass.

$c=\frac{Q}{m\delta T}$,$Q=cm\delta T$
